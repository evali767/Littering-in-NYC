DSNY Volunteer Cleanup Program

The Department of Sanitation (DSNY) offers tools including rakes, shovels, gloves and garbage bags for volunteers to borrow. Community groups can use these tools to clean up local areas. The bagged up trash will be collected by DSNY.

NYC Parks Volunteer Events

NYC Parks and their partners constantly hold events where volunteers can help maintain and improve NYC parks. Several volunteer events are held everyday across the five boroughs.
